Frequently Asked Questions

The Arctic Protocol stands as a groundbreaking DeFi solution, driven by AI, streamlining token launches and granting users the ability to borrow liquidity for trading. Our AI system prioritizes loan requests, evaluates optimal loan amounts, and determines the duration of the lock-in period. Users pay a small processing fee to gain access to liquidity

The protocol loans Liquidity to contract deployers, but maintains hold of the LP tokens after launch. An upfront interest is charged before the loan is disbursed. A variable interest between 5% ~ 10% per month is charged on loans. Majority of minted tokens are usually sent to the Liquidity pool. But if the deployer makes some initial contribution, a reflective amount is sent to their wallet. Learn more from the Docs

Arctic protocol is permissionless, anyone can interact with the smart contract.

We initially propose a variable rate ranging from 5% to 10% per month. This rate will be contingent on demand and market conditions.

Upon deploying the token and adding liquidity on the decentralized exchange, the corresponding LP tokens are retained by the Arctic protocol smart contract until the loan is repaid.

We have commenced partnership negotiations with prominent audit firms, Assure Defi and Certora. They will conduct audits for all smart contracts and protocol applications.
